Brewed with love, served with warmth

Welcome to our cozy corner in the heart of Kampala, where every cup of coffee tells a story. Our expert baristas use the finest locally-sourced beans to deliver a rich, aromatic experience. Whether you're looking for a quick espresso on the go or a relaxing spot to unwind with friends, our coffee shop provides the perfect atmosphere for every occasion.

View Menu Reserve Table

Fresh Coffee

Our coffee is freshly brewed every day using only high-quality beans from local farmers. Each cup is carefully prepared to bring out the rich flavors and aroma that coffee lovers crave. We offer a variety of brewing methods including espresso, pour-over, and French press, ensuring every cup matches your taste preference.

Homemade Pastries

All our pastries are baked in-house daily using fresh ingredients. From flaky croissants to decadent muffins, each treat is crafted to perfectly complement our coffee offerings. We also offer seasonal specials, such as pumpkin spice muffins in autumn and chocolate-stuffed croissants during the holidays.

Organic Beans

We source our coffee beans from sustainable local farms, supporting fair trade and organic farming practices. This ensures not only a superior taste but also a positive impact on the community. Our beans are roasted to perfection, highlighting their natural flavors and unique characteristics, whether you prefer a light, medium, or dark roast.
